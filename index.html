FAQ

Short answers to the questions we hear most.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?
Service businesses and small teams that want clean websites and automations that reduce manual work—without introducing chaos.
Do you use AI everywhere?
Only where it helps. We prioritize reliability and guardrails. If AI adds risk, we use deterministic automation instead.
What tools do you integrate with?
Common stacks like forms, spreadsheets/CRMs, email/SMS providers, and lightweight automation platforms. We keep dependencies minimal.
How fast can we launch?
Most site builds or upgrades take 1–2 weeks. Automation systems vary, but we ship in small increments quickly.
Do you provide documentation and handoff?
Yes—by default. You’ll get a clear summary of what was built, how it works, and how to maintain it.
Can you maintain it for us?
Yes. We offer ongoing iteration and support so your system stays clean as needs change.
What do you need from us to start?
A short description of your current workflow, your goal, and any tools you already use. We’ll handle the rest.
